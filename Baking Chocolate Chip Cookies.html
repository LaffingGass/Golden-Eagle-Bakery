Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

  • 1 cup all purpose flour(250ml)
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda(2ml)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt(2ml)
  • 1/2 cup margarine(125ml)
  • 1/2 cup sugar(125ml)
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar lightly packed(50ml)
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla(5ml)
  • 1 cup chocolate chips(250ml)
  • 1/2 cup walnuts chopped optional(125ml)

Baking Steps

Step 1

  • Sift together flour, baking soda, and salt; set aside.

Step 2

  • Cream together margarine and sugars; add egg, vanilla, and beat until fluffy.`

    • Step 3

    • Stir in dry ingredients, chocolate chips and walnuts(optional)

    Step 4

    • Drop from a teaspoon about 2 inches(5cm) apart onto greased baking sheet.

    Step 5

    • Bake in 350F(180c)oven for 10-12 minutes or until golden.

    Note

    • Makes 35-40 cookies
